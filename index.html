Day 1: The Beginning

4/5/2021

Today marked the beginning of week 2. I guess this means that school's gonna start ramping up again, which is both exciting in that I'm closer to graduating but also exhausting because we didn't get much break. This quarter, the classes I'm taking are:

  • CSE 110
  • CSE 151b
  • ECE 111

I think this quarter's gonna go well, I feel like I got a good balance of classes and the content seems really interesting, so I'm cautiously optimistic about how it'll go. My schedule's pretty great too, I'm pretty much done every day by noon, except with CSE 110 on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Afterwards, I worked out in my cousin's garage where he just finished his home gym setup, which is really cool to essentially have a gym to ourselves. We're currently following a Push-Pull routine as follows:

  1. Pull Day 1
  2. Push Day 1
  3. Legs Day 1
  4. Pull Day 2
  5. Push Day 2
  6. Legs Day 2

I'm excited to start to see what goes on this quarter. I'm gonna try to continue with this workout routine as well and see the results! After my workout, my girlfriend and I went to go to one of my favorite Korean wing spots around me, The Chicken on Fire. I haven't been there since before the pandemic, and I used to go there a lot with my friends from community college. The owner recognized me and my order to my surprise, and even gave us extra wings! Today was a solid day, and I felt that it's a good start to this quarter.

Day 2: Relaxing Day

4/6/2021

I started my day today by attending CSE 151b. It went well, just reviewing stuff from 151a, which is review that was much needed for me. Then, I decided to take a quick nap but with knowing that I needed to pick up my brother at 12:15pm. Luckily, I woke up just in time and went to go pick up my little brother from school, as they just resumed in person learning. After picking up my brother, we went over to my cousin's house where we worked out together. Afterwards, he cooked up some burgers and we decided to watch Zack Snyder's Justice League. My thoughts:

Spoilers I've never seen the Justice League that was released originally by Joss Whedon, so I don't have too much to compare it to, but I thought that this movie was pretty good. I think the DC movies should have followed a model that the Marvel movies did where they took their time and set up each character prior to joining together. Most of the movie was background for Cyborg and Flash, so they definitely did not needed to make a 4 hour movie. Besides that, I thought the movie was cool, had lots of fun with it.
After lunch, my brother and I went home and I attended my CSE 110 lecture. After lecture, I went to my girlfriend's house to hang out and play with our cat, Sol.

She was a stray cat when the rescue found her, so we had to estimate that her birthday was at the end of April, but I can't believe she's already turning 1. I never had a cat before, so this experience has definitely been new to me, but she's a lot of fun and a great companion for the both of us. I'm grateful for her to be in my life, and am excited for when I can be with her full time.
